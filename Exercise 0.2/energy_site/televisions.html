Television Energy Consumption

Modern televisions use far less energy than older models. LED and OLED displays are typically more energy efficient than older plasma or LCD screens.

In Australia, televisions are required to display an energy rating label. This label estimates annual electricity consumption based on typical household usage.

Selecting a TV with a higher energy rating can reduce both energy costs and environmental impact.
