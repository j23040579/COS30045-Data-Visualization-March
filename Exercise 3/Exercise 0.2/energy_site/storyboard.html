Issue : Screen Size Availability and Frequency (56–65 inches most common)

Televisions come in a wide range of screen sizes, but certain size groups are more commonly purchased and used. Based on the findings, TVs in the 56–65 inch range are the most frequent, indicating a strong consumer preference for medium-to-large displays.

TV Size Recommendations for Better Balance

Suggest choosing screen sizes based on room size and viewing distance rather than defaulting to larger TVs. While 56–65 inches is popular, smaller sizes may be more suitable and efficient for compact spaces.
Since 56–65 inch TVs are the most common, recommend selecting models within this range that are optimized for efficiency and viewing quality, rather than simply going larger.
